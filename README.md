## About BruinLoop

BruinLoop is a student utility app designed to make it easier to:
- View UCLA libraries
- Check the availability of private study rooms
- Discover empty classrooms not currently in use

The app is read-only and does not require user accounts or authentication.

---

## Privacy Policy

BruinLoop does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app:
- Does not require user login or accounts
- Does not collect analytics or usage data
- Does not track location or device identifiers
- Does not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs

All information displayed in the app is derived from publicly available campus data and is used solely to display room and space availability.
