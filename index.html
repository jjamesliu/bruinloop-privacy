Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

BruinLoop does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

About BruinLoop

BruinLoop is a UCLA-focused mobile app that helps students find available study spaces, private study rooms, and empty classrooms on campus. The app is designed as a read-only utility that displays publicly available campus information.

What We Don't Collect

BruinLoop is committed to your privacy. The app:

  • Does not require user login or accounts
  • Does not collect analytics or usage data
  • Does not track location or device identifiers
  • Does not use advertising or third-party tracking SDKs
  • Does not store any personal information

Data Sources

All information displayed in BruinLoop is derived from publicly available UCLA campus data and is used solely to display room and space availability in real-time.

Third-Party Services

BruinLoop does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
